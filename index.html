About Me

My name is Tory and I am thankful you have taken the time to read this. I have been married for nearly 12 years and love living in the PNW. My free time is usually spent with my wife, at Providence Park cheering on the Timbers and Thorns, or volunteering. My entire professional career has been focused on helping people. I worked as and EMT assisting people during the worst moments of their lives. Now as a web developer I am looking forward to making user friendly, accessible, and enjoyable applications for every day life.
